Abstract
-----------

In SBOL 2.2.1, the type of a provenance `Activity` is specified by the `roles` of its associated `Usage` objects.
However, this solution does not provide an ideal way for specifying the type of a provenance `Activity`, making it hard to reason over what type of `Activity` transforms `Usage` objects into `wasGeneratedBys` `Identified` objects.
